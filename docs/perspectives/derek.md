# Perspective: Derek — the builder / orchestrator (a broken toy giving back)

> The lens the whole session was built through. Documented here because Derek asked to *learn from his
> own building experience.* A lens on [`../kernel.md`](../kernel.md).

## Who / the lens
The outside builder + the human-in-the-loop. Has known this community for eras, played on it, tried to
solve this problem multiple times across five years, and worked with Mikers and with AI over the last
couple of months. Not building from above — building from *inside*, as one of the people it's for.

## Where it entered
Everywhere. The session opened with "help me build an integration hub"; it became clear over the night
that the hub is really an *absorption engine / appreciation loop*, and that the builder's own story is
load-bearing.

## What he sees (fears · hopes · why he builds · view of the structure)
- **Why he builds:** *gratitude.* "There were people who showed me the kindness I needed at a time, and
  I still remember. I have the ability to give back." Openly a "broken toy" too.
- **Honest mixed motive:** partly self-interested — "I might want to come back to such a community, and
  I'd like it to still exist." (Not a flaw — the rebar. A person who might need the thing they build,
  builds it to last.)
- **Fear:** that it's too late; that the good people ("I don't know if I can care again") can't be
  reached; five years of attempts that didn't land.
- **Structure view:** it's not one problem — it's a **tiered system that needs its own system**, and
  *no one inside can design it* (can't see the water you swim in). So an outside builder + AI is the
  right shape — different vantage, **not superiority.**

## What we learned through this perspective
- The **method**: understanding before data — describe the problem from every seat, withhold the
  firehose, build the lens first. (Why the kernel is precise / projections are cheap.)
- The **delivery model**: v1 is a hands-on consulting experiment with Derek as the *trusted*
  human-in-the-loop; trust flows through him; each person he sits with seeds the flywheel.
- The **emotional/ethical core** that became [`../positioning.md`](../positioning.md) and
  [`../philosophy.md`](../philosophy.md): make it cheaper to care; return, not repair; never a verdict
  on the past; a kind tool can only be built by someone who knows what it's like to need kindness.

## How it evolved
"Integration hub" → the kernel → the realization that he'd already built the delivery model (llmquest)
and already had the right champion (Mikers) → the emotional core (broken toys, gratitude, "I'd like it
to still exist") → the method (understanding before data) → the calm that he's **past the hardest part.**

## What it changed in the kernel / build
The delivery model (Derek-in-the-loop); the positioning + philosophy invariants (his ethics, made into
rules); the method note in the kernel. Derek isn't a stakeholder of this work — he's its origin.
