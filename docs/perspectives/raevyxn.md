# Perspective: Raevyxn — the documentarian (a lens known only by their artifact)

> The lens we met through a spreadsheet, not a conversation — and it mattered anyway. A lens on
> [`../kernel.md`](../kernel.md).

## Who / the lens
A community member who built and maintains the **Era 16 master guild sheet** ("Updated 2025.12.09 by
Raevyxn"). We have no direct words from Raevyxn — only the *artifact*, which speaks clearly. The
meticulous keeper who tries to hold the whole system in one legible place.

## Where it entered
Discovered late, by luck — Derek stumbled on it while browsing the Comfy Discord's "massive amounts of
content" (§20). Landed raw at `../../data/raw/discord-found-sheet.csv`.

## What the artifact shows (inferred lens)
- **Why they (almost certainly) do it:** the same instinct as Mistral — the content *drifts and
  scatters*, so build your own trusted, organized, comprehensive record.
- **Structure view:** the whole guild system captured as a matrix — initiation gates, rank-up
  mechanics, **rewards** (private quarters, boss stones, 10x crafting stations), named achievements,
  and links to each guild's own quest/rank tracker.

## What we learned through this perspective
- The **reward dimension** — the *reasons* you climb, which our rank ladders (the *requirements*) were
  missing: fortresses that grow with rank, boss stones, crafting stations, achievements like
  "Runemaster" and "Ring Bearer." Plus **more ranks** than we'd seen (Explorer's Nomad, Pathfinder).
  (Captured in `../../data/reference/guild-rank-ladders.yaml` → `enrichment`.)
- The pattern, now undeniable: **a *third* independent person building a personal source of truth**
  (after Mistral's Discord and Mikers's ladders). Not a quirk — an **epidemic of personal rafts with no
  harbor.** That *is* the absorption engine's justification.
- The meta-lesson that came with the find: the treasure isn't missing, it's **buried and found only by
  luck.** How many Raevyxn sheets will never be stumbled across? The tool turns *stumbling* into
  *searching.*

## How it evolved
Appeared as a raw find → enriched the guild model → *confirmed the Mistral pattern at scale* → hardened
the case that the community is drowning in its own uncatalogued treasure.

## What it changed in the kernel / build
The guild-model enrichment (rewards, extra ranks, initiation); reinforced the absorption engine's core
justification; a third data point for "everyone builds their own raft." A future kindness: when the
harbor exists, credit and lighten the load of the documentarians who kept the rafts afloat.
