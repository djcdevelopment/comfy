# Perspective: Mikers — Slayer GM, the technical first-mover, the beachhead

> The lens that gave us the data and proved the flywheel. A lens on [`../kernel.md`](../kernel.md).

## Who / the lens
The Slayer GM, and the source of most of the structured data we ingested. Data-fluent, invested, and —
crucially — the *one person who already followed llmquest* and leveled up. The first real beachhead.

## Where it entered
Attributed as the origin of the Base Weapon Stats, the rank ladders, and the weapon-choices data; later
revealed as the llmquest graduate who "will run tutorials."

## What he sees (fears · hopes · why he plays · view of the structure)
- **Why he plays / leads:** systems and data. He *built* the Slayer rank ladders and the weapon-balance
  model by hand — the artifacts we lived in all night.
- **Fear / pain, in his words:** *"ranking — the process is so heavy it comes at the cost of building
  the relationship."* Staff burning out; the tooling "needs to be an extension."
- **Structure view:** already produces structured sources of truth; learns by *integrating a working
  system,* not by tutorial-first (he'll do both, in roughly equal time).

## What we learned through this perspective
- The **ingestion surface**: the first real datasets (weapons, ranks, choices) came through him.
- **Labor-first, validated by the source:** the rank-processing toil is literally crowding out the
  relationship — so the *first recipe* relieves a rep's grind, not a leader's dashboard.
- He's the **ideal beachhead**: data-fluent, invested, in pain, and (via llmquest) already holds the two
  foundational skills — so the rank recipe is *the next quest for a graduate already attuned;* he can run
  it **and build the next one.**
- **The flywheel already turned** — he followed a tool left lying around and became capable. n=1, and the
  1 is exactly the right soil. Validates the whole adoption strategy.

## How it evolved
"Data source" → "Slayer GM" → "first beachhead for the rank recipe" → "llmquest graduate who learns-by-
integrating and propagates (runs tutorials)" → the **first working recipe built for his ladder**
([`../../recipes/rank-ladders/`](../../recipes/rank-ladders/)). The one missing input: his real Slayer
