# Perspective: Mistral — Rangers GM, the volunteer champion, the artist-heart

> The lens that gave us the appreciation loop and the product spec. A lens on
> [`../kernel.md`](../kernel.md).

## Who / the lens
The volunteer — now the Rangers GM. Journeyed player → Mage rep → GM. An artist at heart; the clearest
voice for *why a volunteer volunteers.*

## Where it entered
Identified as "the volunteer"; the journey retold; the private-Discord story; the celebration idea; the
distrust of AI-built bots.

## What she sees (fears · hopes · why she plays · view of the structure)
- **Why she plays / leads:** *"When you can create anything, having things tastes like sand — the joy is
  in providing the experience."* Being a GM mirrors the self; good friends let her be "a version of
  herself she liked." Motivated by *giving others experiences,* never accumulation.
- **Hope:** *"celebrate the people, not just the roles"* — the passion and contribution the org chart
  can't see.
- **Fear:** process/rules colliding with the creative vision; not sure she can care again; distrust of
  handed-down bots.
- **Structure view:** couldn't trust the general docs (they *drifted*), so she built her **own private
  Discord** as a personal source of truth — copied docs, kept notes, sorted her own way, searchable.
  Lost it when she lost Discord access, and **rebuilt it all by hand.**

## What we learned through this perspective
- The **volunteer's intrinsic motivation** (provide experiences, not accumulate) — the champion soul.
- Her private Discord = **the product spec**: people trust their *own owned/organized* capture, not a
  central doc; *the labor is the enemy, not the method.* → owner-controlled, portable-&-owned, the
  absorption engine.
- **"I don't trust [an AI-built bot]"** = wisdom → *no bot, no basement* (a bot is a basement waiting to
  happen; the line is basement, not bot).
- The **celebration** idea (community nomination + tenure-pattern read) → the celebratory feedback loop,
  and ultimately the **Core aim: close the appreciation loop.**
- **"Trained outside the game" / "I'm fun at parties"** → the broken-toys ethos: documentation as a
  survival adaptation; honor it, make the coping lighter, never remove the control that makes it safe.



## What it changed in the kernel / build
Owner-controlled + portable/owned + no-basement invariants; the *celebration* recipe; and the Core aim
itself traces to her. The absorption engine is, at heart, Mistral's private Discord with the toil removed.
